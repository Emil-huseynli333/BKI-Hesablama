The Science Behind Our Calculator

Our BMI calculator is built on the rigorous mathematical standards established by the World Health Organization (WHO) and the National Institutes of Health (NIH). We utilize the standard formula:

BMI = weight (kg) / [height (m)]²

Unlike basic calculators, our platform supports both the Metric system (centimeters and kilograms) and the Imperial system (feet, inches, and pounds), ensuring that no matter where you are in the world, you can receive precise health data without manual conversion errors.
