Who We Are & Our Vision

bkihesablama.site is a passion project developed by a dedicated group of health researchers and software engineers. We noticed that many legacy BMI tools were outdated, difficult to navigate on mobile devices, or overloaded with intrusive advertisements that compromised user experience. Our vision is to become the leading independent provider of body composition analysis tools, serving as a trusted companion for your fitness and wellness goals.

Global Accessibility We provide full support for both Metric and Imperial systems, ensuring that users from any continent can track their health effortlessly without manual conversions.
Data Integrity Our algorithms are rigorously tested against the official Quetelet Index standards, ensuring that every result is clinically sound and reliable for general screening.
Educational Depth We believe in the "Context First" approach. Every tool on our site is accompanied by deep-dive educational content to help you interpret your data correctly.
User Privacy Security is not an afterthought. We utilize client-side processing, meaning your personal measurements never leave your browser or touch our databases.

Our Scientific Foundation

The core of our platform rests on validated medical frameworks. We utilize the Body Mass Index (BMI) parameters established by the World Health Organization (WHO) and the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute (NHLBI). By integrating these global standards into a modern user interface, we offer a bridge between clinical research and daily lifestyle management.

Commitment to Ad-Quality Standards

As a partner of the Google AdSense network, bkihesablama.site is committed to maintaining the highest level of advertising ethics. We ensure that all advertisements served on our platform are non-intrusive and do not interfere with the primary utility of our tools.
