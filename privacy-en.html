Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Protection Overview

At bkihesablama.site, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we handle data and the steps we take to protect your information while you use our BMI calculator and other health tools.

2. Personal Health Data

We do not store or collect the physiological data (height, weight, age) that you enter into our calculator. All calculations are performed instantly within your web browser. This "client-side" processing ensures that your personal biometric data never leaves your device and is never stored on our servers.

3. Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience. We also display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy.

4. Technical Log Files

Like most websites, we collect non-identifiable technical data such as IP addresses, browser types, and entry/exit pages. This information is used solely for analyzing trends and improving the site's performance.
